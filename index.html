Track'N Goal

Help People To Keep Track Of Their Goals

Our flutter-based Goal tracking Web & Mobile App will help individuals and teams set goals and track their progress towards those goals. It will give them a structured way to track and visualize their goal progress through features like dashboards, remainder and settings.

Our Appliaction is available in two modes: Web Application and Mobile Application (Android & iOS devices)

Features

Multiple Sign-Up/Sign-In Options

Our Application offer to users multiple ways to Sign-Up & Sign-In

Users can Sign-Up/Sign-In through three options:

- Email/Password
- Google Account
- Phone Number

Users can choose the desired option in the Sign-Up/Sign-In Pages.

Set Remiders for Goals

Our Application offer to users the possibility to track their goals.

After creating goals, users can set reminders for the specific goals by setting the date and time.

Users can also see the reaminder goals list, edit the date/Time, or delete a remainder goal in settings.

Get Notifications

Our Application offer to users the possibility to recieve notifications from their devices about remainder goals.

Users can activate the notifications in the settings Page

Users can also learn about the application and its licenses by clicking on About button.

About

What inspired us to the project?

People probably have a thousand and one daily tasks and goals that they need to manage. So, how do they set and track all these goals?

Sure, it’s easy to write down their goals and start working towards them, but it’s also easy to forget about them a few weeks, or even days, later which is one of the biggest problems with goals.

Why? Their piece of paper doesn’t have built-in reminders or goal progress tracking features.

So, they need a system that connects their goals to their daily actions and ensures they feel as relevant to their lives on August 20th as they do on January 1st.

Thus, we built a tracking app to help people keep track of their goals and set remainders.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School
